Dataset Overview

Dataset Description

The dataset provides Municipal-level zoning and demographic data for 101 Massachusetts municipalities. Municipalities are denoted by name and id number, and for each municipality, the dataset lists the percentage of single family zoning (compared to other types of housing such as multifamily) as well as whether the municipality is listed on the MBTA communities list. 2010 Census data is used to provide gender, race, and age information at the municipality level. Additionally, this 2010 Census is used to determine information pertaining to the size households and whether households are family or nonfamily. The 2014-2018 census is used to provide data for income brackets at the household level per municipality. I begin by understanding the distribution of the dataset to better understand and assess the quality of the dataset.
